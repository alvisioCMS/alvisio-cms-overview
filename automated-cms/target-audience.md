# Target Audience – Who alvisio is built for

alvisio is designed for organizations where content is created by people who are not trained designers.  
In most companies, websites are maintained by CEOs, assistants, sales teams, educators or marketing staff without formal design education.  
Traditional CMS platforms assume the opposite.

---

## 1. The Problem with Traditional CMS

Systems like WordPress, Wix or Typo3 offer extensive layout freedom:

- drag‑and‑drop elements  
- manual color selection  
- free positioning  
- custom spacing  
- font adjustments  
- complex page builders  

These tools are powerful, but they require **professional design knowledge** to be used correctly.  
In everyday business environments, this leads to:

- inconsistent layouts  
- broken spacing  
- incorrect colors  
- poor typography  
- visual noise  
- high training costs  
- dependency on individual employees  

Even motivated editors struggle to maintain a consistent visual identity.

---

## 2. The Reality in Companies

Most editors:

- are not designers  
- do not know typographic rules  
- cannot judge color harmony  
- do not understand responsive behavior  
- do not want to spend time on layout decisions  

They want to publish content quickly without worrying about design.

alvisio is built exactly for this reality.

---

## 3. The alvisio Approach: Automation Instead of Layout Freedom

alvisio removes the need for design decisions by automating:

- image processing  
- color extraction  
- motif protection  
- heading balancing  
- typographic corrections  
- spacing and layout rules  
- responsive behavior  

Editors only:

- write text  
- upload images  
- publish  

Everything else happens automatically.  
This eliminates errors and ensures consistent quality across all pages.

---

## 4. No Training Required

Because alvisio does not expose complex layout tools, companies benefit from:

- no onboarding time  
- no expensive training  
- no dependency on specific employees  
- easy handover during vacation or staff changes  
- predictable, stable workflows  

New editors can maintain the website within minutes.

---

## 5. Why This Matters

Many users believe they want layout freedom — until they have it.  
In practice, freedom leads to inconsistent results and frustration.

alvisio solves this by providing:

> **Professional design quality without requiring design skills.**

This makes alvisio ideal for organizations that value stability, consistency and efficiency over creative experimentation.
