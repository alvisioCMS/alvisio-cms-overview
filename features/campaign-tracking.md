# Campaign Tracking (Cookie‑free)

alvisio includes a built‑in mechanism for managing and publishing marketing
campaigns across different channels — without using cookies, JavaScript or
external analytics services.

Campaigns can be created, organized and published directly from the
administration interface. They can be grouped into folders, given descriptive
names and enriched with additional information to support structured marketing
workflows.

## Key Capabilities

- organizing campaigns in folders  
- adding names, descriptions and notes  
- preparing campaigns for external platforms  
- publishing campaigns with minimal effort  
- no cookies, no JavaScript, no external services  
- works in all browsers and on all devices  
- fully GDPR‑compliant (no personal data stored)

## Editorial Workflow

Campaigns can be created with a single click — for example directly from a
news article or another content element. Once created, a campaign can be
edited, renamed or supplemented with alternative texts or images.

From within the campaign view, editors can jump directly to their preferred
social‑media platform. alvisio automatically places the prepared campaign
information into the clipboard, allowing the editor to publish the campaign
with just one additional click.

This makes campaign publishing extremely fast and efficient, even for users
without technical knowledge.

## Typical Use Cases

- preparing social‑media posts for website content  
- organizing marketing activities in thematic folders  
- managing QR‑code campaigns for print materials  
- coordinating multi‑channel communication  
- simplifying publication workflows for editors

## Privacy & Compliance

Because the mechanism does not use cookies, does not fingerprint users and does
not store personal data, it is fully compliant with GDPR and other privacy
regulations.

## Benefits

- extremely fast publication workflow (often just two clicks)  
- structured campaign organization  
- no external dependencies  
- works even with strict browser privacy settings  
- ideal for long‑term corporate websites  
- integrates seamlessly into the alvisio architecture
