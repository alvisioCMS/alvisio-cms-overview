
# Automated Design Interpretation of a Professionally Developed Theme

alvisio does not generate design on its own.  
The visual design — typography, spacing, color system, layout rules, image style, and component definitions — is created by **Agentur Albrecht**.

alvisio’s role is fundamentally different from traditional CMS themes:

**alvisio intelligently interprets the design system and applies it automatically to all content.**  
It does not rely on rigid templates.  
It makes context‑aware decisions based on the uploaded content, ensuring that the design remains consistent and visually correct — even when editors have no technical knowledge.

Editors focus entirely on content, while alvisio ensures that the design system is always respected.

---

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Automatic Contrast Optimization in Hero Images
If the design specifies white headlines inside hero images:

- when the uploaded image is bright → alvisio automatically adds a subtle dark overlay  
- when the image is dark → no overlay is applied  

Editors do not need to understand contrast, readability, overlays, or CSS.

---

### 2. Automatic Motif Protection (editor‑defined focal point)
alvisio does not use AI to detect the main subject of an image.  
Instead:

- when uploading an image, the editor clicks on the area that must remain visible  
- alvisio stores this focal point  
- when the design places text boxes left, right, or centered, alvisio positions them so that the protected area is **not covered**

This prevents:

- covered faces  
- hidden logos  
- broken compositions  

No manual cropping or layout adjustments are required.

---

### 3. Automatic Color Harmony for Text Boxes
If the design includes text boxes that overlap hero images:

- alvisio automatically extracts a suitable color from the image  
- chooses white or black text for optimal readability  
- ensures harmonious transitions  
- always follows the design rules defined by the agency  

Editors do not need to understand color theory, contrast ratios, or accessibility.

---

### 4. Automatic Filename Repair on Upload
When a user uploads a file such as: Invitation (2nd version).pdf
alvisio automatically converts it into a web‑safe, SEO‑friendly filename: Invitation_2nd_version.pdf

This ensures:

- browser compatibility  
- clean URLs  
- consistent naming  
- readability for editors  

---

## Why This Is Unique

Traditional CMS themes (WordPress, Typo3, CraftCMS):

- execute templates  
- do not interpret design rules  
- do not make context‑aware decisions  
- do not protect motifs  
- do not optimize contrast or color harmony  
- do not repair filenames  
- require editors to understand layout, image handling, and formatting  

alvisio combines:

- a professionally crafted design system  
- intelligent interpretation  
- automatic application  
- consistent results  
- zero training for editors  

This is a core element of alvisio’s identity as an **Automated CMS**.
